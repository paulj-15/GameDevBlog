ROCKET REBELS

A high-speed action game with adaptive and immersive mechanics.

First Entry

March 20, 2025

Created the first basic prototype for player movement and interactions with basic heatseeker ball mechanics.

Upon hitting the 'F' key the ball bounces back to player 2.

Note: The ball bounces back either way, but if the 'F' key is hit at the right moment, the player does not get launched upwards.

Second Entry

March 30, 2025

Tried out the first ability for the player.

What you see here is the proximity-based teleportation. Much like the agent Omen and his Shrouded Step ability in Valorant.

Note: The teleportation ability as of now, does not have a marker, but it would hopefully be fixed up in the next iteration and entry.

Game Status and Updates:

ROCKET REBELS is a 1v1 duel multiplayer with ability-powered characters. It consists of a ball that is hit to each other with the use of abilities.
