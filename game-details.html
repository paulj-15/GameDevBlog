ROCKET REBELS

A high-speed action game with adaptive and immersive mechanics.

First Entry

March 20, 2025

The first basic prototype for player movement and interactions with basic heatseeker ball mechanics.

I made the heatseeker mechanics target the player, as it was intended in the primary plan.

However, as you can see it led to issues based on the character collision and ball speed.

Note: The ball bounces back either way, but if the 'F' key is hit at the right moment, the player does not get launched upwards. This would be fixed in a later update.

Second Entry

March 30, 2025

Tried out the first ability for the player.

What you see here is the proximity-based teleportation.

Much like the agent Omen and his Shrouded Step ability in Valorant.

Note: The teleportation ability as of now, does not have a marker, but it would hopefully be fixed up in the next iteration and entry.

Third Entry

April 5, 2025

Went further with the ability portion of the character.

This time I took a Shield ability, where the player can block upcoming ball barrages.

Upon stopping the ball, the player can hit it back with the help of the 'F' key

We also made the ball target onto the goal posts behind the player

As it would be much more easier to understand and play around with.

Fourth Entry

April 17, 2025

Added a Charge Attack Mechanic onto the player,

The player can charge his attack using the "C" key and release the charge using the "E" key.

The charge decays over time. So spam that button!!!

I've also added a High Jump/Hover ability for player traversal

We're also planning a grappling ability for later iterations.

Fifth Entry

April 26, 2025

Integration takes time but as our designers ran into some trouble, we had to wait until now to start integration.

The model given to me by our designers were of a robot, so we used that and still have the primitive shapes for shield and the ball, as it's only a prototype.

Wohooo!! I also figured out a way to create a marker for the teleportation ability. It looks cool btw.

Had an opportunity to play with the animation system as well.

During this final week, I will also be trying to support the design team with VFX and SFX for the game.

Sixth Entry

May 3, 2025

Yes, yes. Finally the most exciting part, THE AESTHETICS!!!!

It was fun and honestly kinda challenging to find suitable VFX and SFX for teleportation but nonetheless, I managed to.

Looks kinda cool now huh? Well, it wasn't like this primarly, I spent a while tweaking the VFX to look like what it is now,

Had an opportunity to play with the animation system as well. I also found a well-paired SFX to be triggered at the beginning of the ability.

Moreover, I spent a lot of time with the UI system, as you can see there is a scoring system and a respawn system after a countdown delay.
